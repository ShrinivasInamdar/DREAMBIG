Dreambig

Join us for the fourth edition of DBCE Dream Big – Industry Startup Student Connect, an event dedicated to fostering collaboration between students, industry leaders, and startups.
Dream Big serves as a dynamic platform where students can gain first-hand industry exposure, career insights, and networking opportunities.
Designed to bridge the gap between academia and the professional world, this event enables students to interact with industry experts, explore cutting-edge technologies, and understand real-world industry trends. By engaging with renowned speakers, startup founders, and innovators, participants will gain valuable knowledge beyond textbooks, helping them make informed career choices.
Dream Big is more than just a conference—it’s an experiential learning opportunity that equips students with industry-relevant skills, entrepreneurial inspiration, and a strong professional network. Whether you're a student eager to explore career opportunities, an entrepreneur looking for fresh talent, or a faculty member seeking industry-academic collaboration,
Dream Big 2025 is the event for you.
Join us and take a step toward shaping your future!

Neha Soman

Co-Founder at hypergro.ai, Bengaluru, Karnataka

ABOUT

As a Co-Founder at hypergro.ai, and previously wielding my magic at Glance, I have adventured through the realms of partnerships, content, and campaigns. I believe in the power of stories—every pixel on the screen holds the potential to captivate and connect. This passion has fueled my journey, leading me to spearhead some of Glance's most compelling initiatives. Harmony is key, and partnerships are the melodies that enrich our tune. My ventures with CSK, Sony Music, Warner, and Femina Miss India stand as testaments to the symphonies we create when innovative minds collaborate. Why stop at the ordinary? Venturing into uncharted waters, I conjured up Glance's DeepFake campaign, which blazed across screens with over 260 million views, blending technology with storytelling to captivate millions. I’m not just building brands; I’m in the business of breathing life into visions. It's the thrill of turning the mundane into the mesmerizing and the ordinary into the extraordinary that keeps my cauldron bubbling.

Pavan Allena

Founder at Bower School of Entrepreneurship, Hyderabad, Telangana

ABOUT

Pavan Allena, is the Founder at Bower School of Entrepreneurship - an Undergraduate Business School that focuses purely on building Young Entrepreneurs. Prior to Bower, Pavan founded Metamorphosis in the year 2018. Metamorphosis is the world's first venture that Introduced Entrepreneurship and Financial Literacy for School Children as a part of their academic curriculum. Under Pavan's vision, so far there are more than 100,000 students that enabled with Entrepreneurial Skill Set. In 2023, Metamorphosis got acquired by BrightCHAMPS - a 650 mn USD valued Ed-Tech company. Pavan has been awarded - FORBES INDIA Best People Manager, Times - Promising Entrepreneur of India, and Young Asian Entrepreneur 2019. Pavan loves to play tennis and a big time Roger Federer's Fan. For the next few year's, Pavan is envisioning big to Introduce something bigger in the Entrepreneurial Education space.
